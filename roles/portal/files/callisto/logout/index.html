Logging to Callisto

On a server installation you should use Shibboleth to authenticate. But on a laptop installation, we "mocked" Shibboleth to let you experiment. So, you cannot use the Login/Logout buttons. To mock a Logout:

  • you must be connected to your laptop, inside the Callisto top directory
  • Then type:python3 bin/mockShib.py logout and answer the questions.
